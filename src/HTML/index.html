Welcome to Just Another Microbial Peptide database!

This database aims to be a one-stop database for researchers interested in studying the fascinating field of antimicrobial peptides (AMP).

The AMP data found in this AMP data base is pooled from a few major AMP databases (See other resources for an inexhaustive list of the major sources of AMP information in this database). All efforts have been made to validate the accuracy of the information, and bibliographic information is provided for for interested readers to refer back to the original publication where available.

Of particular interest to the researcher is the BLAST query page, where researchers are able to BLAST against both our AMP database to identify homologues, or against the NCBI BLAST databases to identify potential homologues in other genomes.

We hope that this website will be an invaluable tool for your research!

Specific features of the database includes

  • Links to other AMP databases
  • Identify the genomic coordinates of the genes coding AMP of interest
  • Identify homologues of given AMP sequence in other organisms
  • Identify closest AMP given an unknown sequence
  • Query for an AMP of interest
